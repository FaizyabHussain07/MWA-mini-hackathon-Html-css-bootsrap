Other Projects

Hepatitis

Saylani Welfare has taken an initiative to eliminate Hepatitis from Pakistan. We have set up medical camps in different areas of the country.

Housing Society

Saylani Welfare is providing affordable housing for the poor people. We have built houses for those who don't have shelter.

IT Literacy

Saylani Welfare is providing IT education to the youth of Pakistan. We have set up IT labs in different areas of the country.

Tharparkar

Saylani Welfare is providing food and medical facilities to the people of Tharparkar. We have set up medical camps in different areas of Tharparkar.
